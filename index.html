CONTEXT

CASA VIVA+ is a project or initiative aimed at creating an intelligent home environment, primarily focused on assisting elderly people or those with specific needs. This type of project generally involves the development of assistive technologies to promote greater autonomy and quality of life within the home, allowing users to live more independently and safely.

The Kitchen Assistant was a project previously carried out by LECI students, in which a smart assistant was developed to help elderly people in the kitchen. Its main functions included assisting with recipes, managing pantry items, and creating shopping lists.

OUR PROJECT

The population in many countries, including Portugal, is ageing. Also, there is a decrease of human resources in healthcare. It is essential to invest in prevention, creating conditions for older adults to stay longer in their homes and remain active, but at the same time provide some assistance in their daily life tasks

Our mission is to develop a modular architecture that is easy to integrate into the previous assistant prototypes, capable of intercepting the user's interactions, and dynamically questioning users about these interactions to gather real-time feedback, identify intent, and adapt responses accordingly.

  • MISSION

    Developing a Framework capable of evaluating the Kitchen Smart Assistant dynamically.
