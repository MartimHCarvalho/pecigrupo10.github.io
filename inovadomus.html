ABOUT

InovaDomus is a Portuguese association that promotes innovation and development in the housing sector, with a particular focus on smart and sustainable homes. Comprising companies, higher education institutions, and research centers, Inovadomus aims to foster partnerships and projects that integrate new technologies, energy efficiency, and construction solutions to enhance quality of life and housing sustainability.

The association acts as a collaborative ecosystem, encouraging research and the application of technological solutions in construction and home automation, creating models for homes that are more efficient, safe, and comfortable.
